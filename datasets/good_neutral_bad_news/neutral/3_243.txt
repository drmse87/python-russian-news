GARDAÍ ARE INVESTIGATING after shots were fired at a car in Dublin. A call was received by gardaí of shots fired at Belclare Grove, Ballymun at around 9pm last night. Gardaí attended the scene and a passing car was shot at. The car was located at Belclare Terrace with damage.  There have been no reports of anyone injured.
