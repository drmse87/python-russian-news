Israel: Hamas began with 7 dead, they now have 21
Itamar Eichner, Elior Levy|Published:  11.13.18 , 20:24
An Israeli official commented on the latest escalation in Gaza saying: “Hamas began this phase of fighting with seven dead and ended it with 21 dead.”

Hamas released a statement saying: “Israel learnt a tough lesson and paid a price for its crimes. Its leaders are confused and they were forced to step back.”
